Who We Are

Integrated excellence from soil to table

Founded in Ogun State, OGAS Integrated Services bridges agriculture and food service — we grow quality produce and turn it into memorable dining experiences for our clients.

Our team combines agronomic expertise, culinary skill, and entrepreneurial drive to create lasting value for farmers, communities, and event clients across Nigeria.

🌍 Sustainability

Intercrop systems that protect soil, conserve water, and improve yield over time.

📦 Quality

Every product — fresh or prepared — meets consistent standards before delivery.

🚜 Local First

We source inputs locally and return value to Ogun State farming communities.

🔬 Research-Backed

Our practices are informed by current agronomy science and seed technology.
